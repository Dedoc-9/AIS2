# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

AIS² is committed to providing a welcoming, inclusive, and respectful environment for all contributors, regardless of age, background, experience, gender identity and expression, level of experience, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, sexual identity and orientation, or socioeconomic status.

We are dedicated to fostering a community where everyone feels safe to contribute, participate, and voice their ideas.

---

## Our Standards

### Behavior We Encourage

- **Be respectful** — Treat others with courtesy and professionalism
- **Be inclusive** — Welcome diverse perspectives and experiences
- **Be constructive** — Provide thoughtful feedback; focus on the work, not the person
- **Be patient** — Recognize that understanding takes time
- **Be collaborative** — Work together toward shared goals
- **Assume good intent** — Interpret ambiguous statements charitably

### Examples of Positive Behavior

- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being respectful of differing opinions, viewpoints, and experiences
- Giving and receiving constructive criticism gracefully
- Focusing on what is best for the community
- Showing empathy towards other community members
- Crediting the work of others appropriately

### Behavior We Do Not Tolerate

- Harassment, discrimination, or intimidation of any kind
- Offensive language or conduct (including slurs, insults, or belittling)
- Unwelcome sexual attention or advances
- Personal attacks or ad hominem arguments
- Trolling, deliberate disruption, or bad-faith engagement
- Doxxing or sharing private information without consent
- Any other conduct that violates laws or community norms

---

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies in all AIS² spaces, including:

- GitHub repository (issues, pull requests, discussions, wiki)
- Email communication
- Documentation
- Discord/Slack/forums (if established)
- Conference talks or meetups representing AIS²
- Any official AIS² event or space

It applies to all contributors, maintainers, and community members.

## Reporting & Enforcement

### How to Report

If you witness or experience a violation of this Code of Conduct:

1. **Contact the maintainer directly**:
   - Email: bigdilly95@gmail.com
   - Subject: "Code of Conduct Violation"

2. **Do NOT post publicly** (unless the person's safety is at immediate risk)

3. **Include**:
   - Description of the incident
   - When and where it occurred
   - Who was involved
   - Any relevant links or screenshots
   - What impact it had on you

### Confidentiality

All reports are handled confidentially. We will not publicly name the reporter unless they consent.

### Investigation & Response

1. **Receipt** — Acknowledge report within 48 hours
2. **Investigation** — Gather information from relevant parties
3. **Determination** — Assess whether a violation occurred
4. **Action** — Take appropriate remedial action
5. **Follow-up** — Check in with the reporter on resolution

### Possible Outcomes

Depending on severity:
- **Warning** — Private conversation about expected behavior
- **Temporary suspension** — Revoked access for a period (1 week – 3 months)
- **Permanent ban** — Permanent removal from the AIS² community

**Serious violations** (harassment, threats, doxxing) may result in permanent ban on first offense.
